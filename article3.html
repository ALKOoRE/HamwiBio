Multifocal Intra Ocular Lenses

April 9 2025

Latest in Ophthalmic Surgery ( April 2025)

Modified Intraocular Lenses (IOLs)
The traditional kind of intra ocular lenses was designed to replace the natural opaque lens in Cataract Surgery, but now they are modified, they are designed not just for cataract, but to correct refractive errors like Myopia, Hyperopia, Presbyopia and Astigmatism as well.

Multifocal lenses, particularly trifocal IOLs are now giving the patient a wider range of vision field and an extra sharp acuity of vision.

They give advanced aspheric option that improves image quality and increases contrast sensitivity.

This technology means that all refractive errors patients will get a Crystal-Clear Vision at all distances, they do not now have to switch between lenses or glasses.
