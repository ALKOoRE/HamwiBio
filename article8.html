June 22 2026

Currently at Al Hamwi BioMedical Research Center. Throughout our journey, which began in 2008, we have been able to innovate and develop several vital technologies, which have been globally accredited. The most prominent of these innovations are:
* Therapy of the intractable disease Retinitis Pigmentosa, which has baffled scientists for decades, is now being developed through the Re-Sequence of Nitrogenous Bases in DNA, thereby correcting the genetic mutations in five human genes associated with the retina.
* Early detection of the most serious and complex diseases affecting humans, including cancerous and silent diseases:
OCT Based, Disease Early Detection.
This is achieved by analyzing the OCT Retinal Scan in just 7 seconds and linking it to algorithms for Pathological Signs, thus detecting the disease before it develops.
* Using the eye cornea of a rare South African Black Neck Ostrich breed, after genetically modifying this bird to be a replacement for a damaged human eye cornea.
Dr. Morhaf Al Hamwi Founder, Ophthalmologist, the Royal College of Ophthalmologists member
London, United Kingdom This center was established in 2008, We utilize the latest therapeutic technologies globally, in addition to finding a radical solutions for the most complex cases that made scientists baffled for decades.
Throughout our history, we have provided services to more than 30 countries worldwide.
Our medical research focus on developing healthcare through the innovation, advanced diagnostic methods, and a deeper understanding of the causes of diseases.
We translate the laboratory findings into a practical applications in the operating rooms in hospitals, surgical authorities and in clinics.
We also train and develop researchers, professional surgical skills and medical students.
