Flexible Artificial Retina 2026

May 30 2026

A "Flexible Artificial Retina" has been developed: "BioElectrical Retina", which responds to light stimulation. Here, we observe a tangible change in the retinal function on Electro Retino Graphy (ERG).
And the SubRetinal Photo-Voltaic Microchips receive Near-InfraRed (NIR) light to treat the central vision loss such as in Age-related Macular Degeneration (AMD) case.
These implants convert light into electrical signals to stimulate the remaining nerve cells, enabling patients to regain high-resolution functional central vision.


Good news for retinal patients. Regardless of the extent of retinal damage...
"Flexible Artificial Retina Implantation" - a new scientific revolution...
British specialists have developed an advanced technology for implanting a retinal replacement for the damaged retina. This new retina guarantees restored vision with high acuity and an expanded field of vision, all compared to the surgical methods used until now.

  • Vision loss is most often due to the death of light-sensitive cells or sensory receptors, namely the rods and cones in the retina. The best and most modern method for treating this condition is Artificial Retina Implantation.

  • The new retina is distinguished by its simple implantation; it is wire-free, made of highly flexible materials with PhotoElectric Pixels, and allows the user to expand their field of vision by an additional 60 degrees.
    This technology is powered by Solar Energy, which stores the necessary energy for the retina's operation automatically and continuously.

The British company "Optical Express", which is developing this project, is committed to keeping the cost affordable and accessible to any patient with minimal financial burden, and aims to make it available in most of the world countries within a few months.
