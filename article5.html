OCT Based, Disease Early Detection 2026

January 1 2026

Our team has developed a pioneering technology:
OCT-Based Disease Early Detection 2026

This groundbreaking technology enables specialists to detect 180 diseases early, including the most serious and dangerous illnesses affecting the human body, such as cancerous tumors and silent diseases (before the patient himself is even aware of their presence).

We have developed AI-Powered algorithms which were built and developed by sequencing and replicating information extracted from the retinal images Optical Coherence Tomography (OCT) stored in our own records, research and medical camps records, this vast amount of information was linked to a previous pathological signs, these algorithms will analyze the (OCT) image of the retina in just 7 seconds.

Therefore, we can determine the most effective, rapid, and successful treatment for these conditions.

We studied a random sample of 140 people from Europe, America, Asia and Africa.
After analyzing their OCT retinal images, we found that 112 of them had already begun a journey through various major diseases, most of which were chronic and silent, without their knowledge.

The great importance of this technology lies in its ability to enable specialists to treat the disease easily upon its early detection (Disease Early Detection), before the patient's body begins to collapse should their condition worsen, especially if the disease is silent or malignant.

This technology is expected to benefit more than 140 million patients worldwide during the coming years.
